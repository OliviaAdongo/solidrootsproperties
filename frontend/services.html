What We Do

We offer a comprehensive range of real estate services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

1. Residential Agency
The Residential Agency division offers comprehensive services for buyers, sellers, and investors in the residential real estate market, including sales, leasing, market analysis, and advisory services.
2. Commercial & Retail Agency
We help businesses find suitable commercial properties for growth, expansion, and profitability. We offer leasing and sales services, investment property analysis, and market research to guide smart investment decisions.
3. Real Estate Investment
Our Investment Division offers clients strategic real estate investments, strategy development, and market trends analysis, ensuring they are well-positioned to capitalize on new opportunities and meet their financial goals.
4. Property & Facility Management
Our property management services include tenant placement, common area management, maintenance, property oversight, rent collection, and financial reporting, ensuring quality occupants and transparent financial reporting to property owners.
5. Occupier Services
Our occupier services assist businesses in finding and managing suitable properties, managing portfolios to boost company success, and fostering tenant relations to ensure high-quality property maintenance, promoting tenant satisfaction and long-term tenancy.
6. Valuation & Advisory
Our valuation and advisory services provide accurate property appraisals and expert advice on various real estate matters, including sales, purchase decisions, mortgages, insurance, and investments, as well as corporate restructuring and public listings.
7. Urban Planning, Master Planning & Research
Our urban planning services assist developers and municipalities in creating sustainable communities, focusing on eco-friendly plans aligned with Kenya's growth goals, and offering expert guidance on project design and concepts.
